About The Day Care School

Maembe Day Care is a reputable child care center that has been providing quality care and education to young children for several years. We are dedicated to creating a safe and nurturing environment where children can learn, grow, and thrive. Our team of experienced caregivers and educators are passionate about fostering the development of each child in our care, ensuring that they receive the attention and support they need to reach their full potential.

Our History

Maembe Day Care was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate individuals who recognized the need for high-quality child care in the community. Starting with just a few children, the day care center has grown significantly over the years, expanding its facilities and services to accommodate more families and provide a wider range of programs for children of all ages.

Maembe day acre was established to provide quality, care and eduacation to young children in a safe environment.
